About This Guide

Holy City Cafe Guide is a recommendation website. We're an independent guide to the best cafes in Charleston, SC — the spots worth going to for a real breakfast, brunch, or lunch, not just a cup of coffee. We're not affiliated with, owned by, or operated by any of the cafes listed here.

Our picks are based on local reputation, reviews, and general knowledge of Charleston's cafe scene. Menus, hours, and offerings can change, so we always recommend checking with the cafe directly before you go.

Think of this as a friendly starting point for finding your next favorite table in the Holy City — not a booking service, just a guide.
